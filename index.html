Lonavala - Maharashtra
Lonavala places to visit include the Tiger's Leap, Amrutanjan Point etc which offer scenic views of the Sahyadri hills, forested lands and golden sunsets. Moreover, places like Rajmachi Fort, Tikona Fort, Lohagarh Fort and more are ideal for trekking and hiking along with their admirable architecture.

Harihar Fort - Nashik, Maharashtra
Harihar fort or Harihar killa is located in the Trimbakeshwar Mountain Range. The Yadava Dynasty constructed the fort between the 9th and 14th centuries. This fort in Maharashtra has held great importance in preventing trade routes passed through the Gonda Ghat.

View more information about Harihar Fort

Tadoba-Andhari Tiger Reserve - Chandrapur, Maharashtra
The Tadoba Andhari Tiger Reserve is a wildlife sanctuary in Chandrapur district of Maharashtra state in India. It is Maharashtra's oldest and largest national park. Created in 1955, the reserve includes the Tadoba National Park and the Andhari Wildlife Sanctuary.
